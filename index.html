About Us

At Contenta, we aim to design a mobile application that detects potentially harmful substances in packaged food products. Users can use their mobile phone's camera to scan the ingredients section. By personalizing their experience with their allergy information, height, and weight, our application identifies allergens in the product content and potential complications. We utilize advanced language processing algorithms to ensure accurate scans, even with wrinkled or folded packages. Initially available in Turkish and English, we plan to expand to more languages through a simple translation API.
