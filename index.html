Contenta

We aim to design a mobile application that detects potentially harmful substances (such as aspartame, monosodium glutamate or E621) in the packaged food products by scanning the ingredients section with their mobile phone’s camera. Users will have a chance to personalize their experience if they enter their information about their allergies, height, and weight. Thus, our application will be able to identify which substances in the product content are allergens and what type of complications they may cause to the user. We are also considering using appropriate language processing algorithms to prevent faults (lack or misinterpretation of letters) during scanning process due to wrinkles and folds on the packages. Although we initially planning to develop our application in Turkish and/or English, we intent to provide this service for many popular languages with a simple translation API.
